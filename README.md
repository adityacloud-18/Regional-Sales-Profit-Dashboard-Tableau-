## Overview
This project showcases an **interactive Tableau dashboard** created to analyze and visualize key business metrics from the **Superstore dataset**. The dashboard highlights insights on **regional sales performance**, **sub-category profitability**, and **customer distribution** across different locations.

---

## Objectives
- Understand regional performance trends.  
- Identify top-performing and underperforming product categories.  
- Visualize customer sales patterns using interactive filters and maps.  
- Support data-driven decision-making through intuitive visuals.

---

## Key Insights
- **West Region** generated the highest total sales.  
- **Copiers** and **Accessories** were the most profitable sub-categories.  
- **Binders** showed negative profit margins, requiring pricing strategy review.  
- Customer distribution was highest in major metropolitan areas, as shown on the heat map.
